Welcome to Egypt

The great Nile and magnificent architecture, the beguiling desert and lush delta, and with its long past and welcoming, and story-loving people are heart warming and Egypt welcomes you with all these amazing sites. Egypt alone had ruled a very strong empire from Cairo (AL Qahira). The very populated city is packed with mosques, churches, soaring minarets and medieval schools, and some of the greatest architecture of medieval Islam and Egypt's native Coptic Orthodox Christians. The Coptic Orthodox Christians have carried on their traditions such as the church’s liturgical language and the traditional calendar which do link back to the pharohs time. Tap into the history in Cairo's early churches and in remote desert monasteries.


Food


Flavours of Egypt

Egyptian cuisine is often known for its dramatic flavours such as dishes that include ful medames which is mashed fava beans, kushari that is a mixture of lentils, rice, pasta, salsa, and other great and flavour filled ingredients, feteer meshaltet, baklava, and many more dishes you propably have never heard of. Egypt's unique cuisines are not as fancy as italian or french cuisines but Egyptians do put a bold touch on the food with the spices which make Egyptian food very tasty. Much of the types of food rely heavily on vegetables and legumes rather than meats. Each region in Eygypt has its own distinctive tastes and dishes which make Egyptian cuisine even more unique.

Best Experiences in Egypt

Pyramids, Beaches, and Beyond

With sand-covered tombs, austere pyramids and towering Pharaonic temples, Egypt brings out the explorer in all of us. Visit the Valley of the Kings in Luxor, where Tutankhamun’s tomb was unearthed, and see the glittering finds in the Egyptian Museum in Cairo. Hop off a Nile boat to visit Dendara, Edfu or one of the other waterside temples, cross Lake Nasser to see Ramses II’s masterpiece at Abu Simbel, or trek into the desert to find the traces of Roman trading outposts. You never know – your donkey might stumble across yet another find, for that is the way many previous discoveries were made.


That empty beach with nothing but a candlelit cabin, and a teeming coral reef offshore: they’re waiting for you in Egypt. The coast along the Red Sea has a rugged desert beauty above the waterline and a psychedelic vibrancy below – rewarding to explore on a multiday outing to one of the globe’s great dives or on an afternoon’s snorkelling jaunt along a coral wall. There is even more space and just as much beauty in Egypt’s vast deserts. Whether you’re watching the sun rise between the beautiful shapes of the White Desert or the shimmering horizon from the comfort of a hot spring in Siwa Oasis, Egypt’s landscapes are endlessly fascinating.
