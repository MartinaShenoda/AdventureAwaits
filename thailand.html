Welcome to Thailand

Sacred Temples

Thailand's religious devotion is colourful and it is known for its Buddhist nations. Thailand has glistening temples and golden Buddhas, these magnificent sites frame the landscape. Ancient banyan trees are ceremoniously wrapped in sacred cloth to honour the resident spirits, fortune-bringing shrines decorate humble homes as well as monumental malls, while garland-festooned dashboards ward off traffic accidents. Visitors get to experience different meditation retreats in Chiang Mai, religious festivities in northeastern Thailand, explore cave sanctuaries within Phetchaburi and Kanchanaburi, and visit holy temples on hilltops of northern Thailand.


Tropical Landscapes

Even though Thailand has cluttered cities and towns, you can still explore the landscapes that lie beyond these areas such as tropical forests and squat villages. The forests and fields can be found in northern Thailand that lay against toothy blue mountains that have silvery waterfalls. In the south, scraggy limestone cliffs poke out of the cultivated landscape like prehistoric skyscrapers. The usually arid northeast emits an emerald hue during the rainy season when tender green rice shoots carpet the landscape.


Lively Beaches

Thailand is a tropical getaway for the hedonist and the hermit, the prince and the pauper. This paradise offers a varied menu: playing in the gentle surf of Ko Lipe, diving with whale sharks off Ko Tao, scaling the sea cliffs of Krabi, kiteboarding in Hua Hin, partying on Ko Phi Phi, recuperating at a health resort on Ko Samui and feasting on the beach wherever sand meets sea.
